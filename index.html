Who is Kim Taeyeon?

Taeyeon was a trainee at the biggest Entertainment Company in South Korea named S.M. Entertainment. She trained until she debuted in one of the biggest South Korean girl groups of all time named Girls Generation. Girls Generation was a nine member group that debuted in 2007. At one point during her trainee days, she wanted to quit because the training was so tough but she never gave up and continued to follow her dreams of becoming a singer. She also was in a sub-unit with two other members of the group and in 2015 she debuted as a solo artist with her debut mini album, "I." She is still making music till this day until her contract expires in 2024. Once her contract ends, she will have to choose to continue her career, or to retire. She is known as being one of the most popular female solo artists in South Korea.

Childhood and Early Life

  • She was born March 9, 1989 in Jeonju, South Korea. She grew up with her older brother and younger sister. From an early age, she relized the one thing she would excel at is singing which her dad was a little hesitant at first.
  • Taeyeon soon started her training at SM Academy.
  • Three years after her training she signed her first contract with SM Entertainment even before she finished her training.

Career

  • Taeyeon was part of a girl group called "Girl's Generation" which she debuted with in August 2007.
  • There first single, Gee, released in 2009.
  • Taeyeon made her name known by doing side projects like recording soundtracks for various dramas that hit the charts. She even got the nickname "ost queen".
  • In 2012, she debuted in a sub-unit called "TTS" with two other group mates, Tiffany and Seohyun.
  • In 2015, she finally debuted as a solo artist with her hit single, "I". Since then, she been toping the music charts.
